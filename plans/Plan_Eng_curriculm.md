## Curriculum Output

### English Curriculum — Early Learner (Age 5)
**Entry Level:** Knows phonics, can blend CVC (2–3 letter) words
**Duration:** 12 weeks (adaptable)

---

#### Module 1 — Vocabulary Expansion (Weeks 1–3)

| Week | Theme | Target Words (10/week) | Activity |
|------|-------|------------------------|----------|
| 1 | Animals & Habitats | lion, tiger, whale, parrot, turtle, spider, eagle, rabbit, camel, dolphin | Flashcard matching, "What animal am I?" game |
| 2 | Food & Meals | breakfast, vegetable, sandwich, dessert, noodles, salad, biscuit, orange, mango, soup | Grocery store role-play, draw-and-label |
| 3 | Feelings & Emotions | happy, sad, angry, surprised, tired, excited, nervous, proud, bored, confused | Emotion mirror game, "How do you feel?" journal |

**Skill target:** Recognise and use 30 topic words in spoken sentences.

---

#### Module 2 — Sentence Building (Weeks 4–6)

| Week | Sentence Pattern | Example |
|------|-----------------|---------|
| 4 | Subject + Verb | "The dog runs." / "Birds fly." |
| 5 | Subject + Verb + Object | "I eat an apple." / "She reads a book." |
| 6 | Subject + Verb + Object + Place/Time | "We play in the park." / "He sleeps at night." |

**Activities:**
- Sentence strips: cut up words and reassemble in order
- "Finish my sentence" partner game
- Daily dictation (1 sentence read aloud, child writes)

**Skill target:** Build and write 4–5 word grammatically correct sentences independently.

---

#### Module 3 — Reading Comprehension (Weeks 7–9)

| Week | Text Level | Comprehension Focus | Strategy |
|------|-----------|--------------------|-|
| 7 | Simple decodable readers (CVC+CVCE) | Who, What, Where | Read aloud → point & answer |
| 8 | Level 1 picture books | Sequencing (first/next/last) | Retell with picture prompts |
| 9 | Short paragraphs (3–4 sentences) | Main idea + 1 detail | "Somebody-Wanted-But-So-Then" frame |

**Skill target:** Answer 3 comprehension questions correctly after one reading.

---

#### Module 4 — Speaking Confidence (Weeks 10–12)

| Week | Activity | Goal |
|------|----------|------|
| 10 | Show & Tell (favourite toy/book) | Speak 3+ sentences without prompting |
| 11 | Story retelling (familiar book) | Use sequence words: first, then, after, finally |
| 12 | Mini presentation: "My favourite animal" | 5 sentences, eye contact, clear voice |

**Scaffolds:** sentence starter cards ("I think…", "First…", "My favourite…")
**Assessment:** teacher checklist — fluency, vocabulary, confidence (1–3 scale)

---

#### Assessment Milestones

| Milestone | Criteria | Method |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| End of Module 1 | Uses 20+ new words correctly in speech | Oral vocabulary check |
| End of Module 2 | Writes 3 correct sentences independently | Written sample |
| End of Module 3 | Answers Who/What/Where from a paragraph | Comprehension quiz (verbal) |
| End of Module 4 | Speaks for 30+ seconds on a topic | Recorded mini-presentation |
